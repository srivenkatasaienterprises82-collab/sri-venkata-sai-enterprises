# How to Edit Your Website — Simple Guide for Non-Technical Users

This guide explains, in plain language, how to change the text, products, prices,
photos, and other content on your website **without any technical knowledge**.

You do NOT need to write code. You only use a simple online tool called the
**Studio** (also known as the "CMS" or content editor).

---

## ⚡ Short Version (Quick Steps)

1. Open **https://www.srivenkatasaienterprises.com/studio** in your browser.
2. Log in with the Sanity account (email/password or Google).
3. You will see a list of content types (Products, Banners, FAQs, etc.).
4. Click the item you want to change → edit the boxes → click **Publish** (top right).
5. Wait a few seconds, then refresh your website — the change is live.

That's it. The rest of this document explains each step in more detail.

## 1. Open the Editing Tool (the "Studio")

- Go to: **https://www.srivenkatasaienterprises.com/studio**
- Bookmark this page so you can return to it easily.
- Use a computer (laptop/desktop) for the best experience. A phone works but is harder.

## 2. Log In

- You will see a login screen.
- Sign in with the Sanity account that was given to you (the same email used to
  create the website content).
- If you forgot the password, use "Forgot password" on the login screen, or ask
  the developer to reset it.

## 3. The Screen — What You Are Looking At

After login you see a menu on the left (or a search box at the top). This menu
lists all the types of content on your website, for example:

| Menu Item | What it controls on the website |
|---|---|
| **Products** | The phones/items shown in the shop |
| **Brands** | The brand logos (Apple, Samsung, Vivo, etc.) |
| **Categories** | Groups like "New Arrival", "Best Seller" |
| **Banners** | The big promotional boxes on the home page |
| **Testimonials** | Customer review quotes |
| **FAQ** | The "Frequently Asked Questions" page |
| **Gallery** | Photos of your store |
| **Offers** | Deal boxes (EMI, Exchange, etc.) |
| **Pages** | About Us, Privacy, Terms, Shipping pages |
| **Site Settings** | Your shop name, phone, address, hours, social links |
| **Home Page** | Which products appear in the home page sections |

To find something fast, use the **Search** box at the top and type the name
(e.g. "iPhone", "Samsung").

## 4. How to Edit Something That Already Exists

Example: change the price of a product.

1. Click **Products** in the menu.
2. Find the product (use Search if there are many).
3. Click its name to open it.
4. Change the text in the boxes you want to update:
   - **Price (₹)** — type the new number.
   - **Original Price (₹)** — the "was" price (leave empty if none).
   - **Name, Description, Stock**, etc.
5. When finished, click the **Publish** button at the top-right.
6. Open your website and refresh — the new price shows.

> 💡 **Important:** Just typing in the box is NOT enough. You MUST click
> **Publish**, otherwise your change is only saved as a draft and the website
> will not update.

## 5. How to Add a New Product

1. Click **Products** in the menu.
2. Click the **"Create"** (or **+ New Product**) button (usually top-right).
3. Fill in the boxes:
   - **Name** — e.g. "Samsung Galaxy S26".
   - **Slug** — this is the web address ending. Type a short name with no spaces,
     e.g. `samsung-galaxy-s26`. (You can leave it and it will fill automatically.)
   - **Type** — e.g. `smartphone`.
   - **Stock** — choose `in-stock`, `out-of-stock`, or `pre-order`.
   - **Price (₹)** and **Original Price (₹)**.
   - **Brand** — click and pick the brand from the list (Apple, Samsung…).
   - **Category** — click and pick a category.
   - **Cover Image URL** — see Section 6 (Images).
   - **Description** — write a short description.
   - **Featured** — turn ON if you want it shown prominently on the home page.
4. Click **Publish**.
5. The product now appears on the website.

To **delete** a product: open it → menu (⋯ or the dots) → **Delete** → confirm.
(Only delete if you are sure — deleted items cannot be easily recovered.)

## 6. How to Add or Change Images (Photos)

> ⚠️ **Read this carefully.** In this website, a photo is added by pasting a
> **link (web address) of the image**, not by clicking an "upload" button.

### Option A — Use a photo that is already on the internet
1. Right-click the picture on any website → choose **"Copy image address"**
   (or "Copy image link").
2. In the Studio, paste that link into the image box (e.g. **Cover Image URL**).
3. Publish.

### Option B — Use your own photo (from your phone/computer)
You have two simple choices:

- **Easy way:** Upload your photo to a free image-hosting site (for example
  ImgBB, PostImage, or Google Photos "Get shareable link"), copy the link that
  ends in `.jpg` / `.png` / `.webp`, and paste it into the image box. Then Publish.
- **Best way (recommended for product photos):** Send the photo to the developer.
  They will place it on the website's image folder. After that, you type the
  path they give you, for example: `/images/products/samsung-s26/1.webp`,
  into the image box, and Publish.

> 📌 The image boxes are labeled things like **Cover Image URL**, **Logo URL**,
> **Image URL**, or **Images**. Just paste the link/path there.

### Good photo tips
- Use clear, bright photos.
- Square or landscape photos look best.
- Keep file size reasonable (under a few MB) so the page loads fast.

## 7. How to Edit the Other Pages

- **About Us / Privacy / Terms / Shipping:** Click **Pages** → open the page →
  edit the text → **Publish**.
- **FAQ (Questions & Answers):** Click **FAQ** → open or create an item → fill
  **Question** and **Answer** → Publish. Use the **Order** number to control
  the sequence (1, 2, 3…).
- **Testimonials (Reviews):** Click **Testimonials** → edit the customer name,
  quote, and star **Rating** (1–5) → Publish.
- **Banners (Home page promo boxes):** Click **Banners** → edit title, subtitle,
  button text, link, and image → set **Active** ON to show it → Publish.
- **Offers (Deal boxes):** Click **Offers** → edit title, badge, discount text,
  image, link → choose a **Type** → Publish.
- **Gallery (Store photos):** Click **Gallery** → add/edit image links and
  captions → Publish.
- **Site Settings (Your shop info):** Click **Site Settings** → update:
  - Company name, short name, tagline
  - Phone number, WhatsApp number, email
  - Address, city, pincode, opening hours
  - Social media links (Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn)
  - Footer text and copyright text
  - SEO title/description (used by Google)
  Then **Publish**. This information appears across the whole website.

## 8. Publish — The Most Important Step

Every time you finish editing:

1. Look at the top-right of the screen.
2. Click **Publish**.
3. The change is sent to the live website.

If you see a button that says **"Publish"** in green/blue, click it.
If it says **"Updated"** or **"Saved"** with no Publish button, the item is
already published.

After publishing, go to your website and **refresh the page** (press F5 or
Ctrl+R) to see the update.

## 9. Things to Be Careful About

- ✅ Always click **Publish** — drafts are not visible to customers.
- ✅ Don't change the **Slug** of an existing product unless you know what it
  does (it changes the web address).
- ✅ Don't delete items you are unsure about.
- ✅ For the **Brand** and **Category** boxes, pick from the list — don't type
  a new name there, or the connection will break.
- ❌ Don't paste anything into the image boxes except an image link/path.
- ❌ Don't change technical-looking fields you don't understand (they are usually
  already set correctly).

## 10. When to Ask the Developer

Please contact the developer for:

- Uploading many product photos at once.
- Adding a completely new type of page or section.
- Changing colors, layout, fonts, or the design of the website.
- Connecting a new payment or delivery system.
- If something looks broken after an edit (they can restore it).

For everyday text, price, photo-link, and content updates — you can do it
yourself using this guide. 🎉

## Quick FAQ

**Q: I edited something but the website didn't change.**
A: You probably forgot to click **Publish**. Open the item again, click Publish,
then refresh the website.

**Q: Where do I log in?**
A: https://www.srivenkatasaienterprises.com/studio

**Q: Can I upload a photo directly from my computer inside this tool?**
A: Not directly. Paste an image link (see Section 6), or send the photo to the
developer to add it for you.

**Q: I made a mistake. Can I undo it?**
A: Open the item, check the history/versions if available, or ask the developer
to restore the previous version.

**Q: Will my changes break the website?**
A: Normal text/price/photo edits are safe. Avoid deleting items and avoid
editing technical fields you don't recognize.
